# Grandma's Apple Pie Recipe

## Ingredients
- 6 cups sliced apples
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- Dash of salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Pastry for double-crust pie

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F
2. Mix sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt
3. Arrange half the apples in pastry-lined pie plate
4. Sprinkle with half the sugar mixture
5. Repeat layers
6. Dot with butter
7. Cover with top crust
8. Bake 40-50 minutes until golden brown

Enjoy with vanilla ice cream!
